## üîÅ Special: Send `CTRL+A` to the terminal

To send the actual `CTRL+A` keystroke (e.g., to go to the beginning of the line in Bash), press:

`CTRL+A` twice:  
`LEADER` + `CTRL+A` (hold `Ctrl`, press `A` twice)
